❓ FAQ

Questions? We've got answers.

Everything you need to know about StatusGuard, compliance monitoring, and protecting your visa status.

How is StatusGuard different from my DSO or university portal?

▼

Your DSO sees your data after it's already filed to SEVIS — often too late to fix issues. StatusGuard mirrors that view in real-time and alerts you before problems are reported. Think of it as your early warning system, not a replacement for your DSO.

Is my visa and financial data actually safe?

▼

Absolutely. All OCR processing happens locally on your device — your I-20 and visa documents never leave your phone. Financial data is encrypted with AES-256, and we use Plaid's bank-grade security. We're FERPA-compliant and SOC 2 Type II certified.

What happens if I miss a compliance deadline?

▼

StatusGuard sends escalating alerts via push notifications, SMS, and email. If you're on the Premium plan with Deactivation Defense Insurance, you'll have access to emergency legal support and repatriation coverage if a violation occurs.

Does StatusGuard work with all universities?

▼

Yes. We integrate with major student information systems like Terra Dotta, Sunapsis, and iStart. If your university uses a custom portal, you can manually sync your data or upload documents via our OCR engine.

Can I use this for OPT or CPT tracking?

▼

Currently, StatusGuard focuses on F-1 student status compliance. OPT/CPT tracking and H-1B transition planning are on our roadmap for Q3 2026. Join our waitlist to get early access when these features launch.

What if my bank isn't supported by Plaid?

▼

You can manually enter your balance and update it weekly. We'll still monitor your liquidity against I-20 requirements and send alerts. Plaid supports 12,000+ institutions, including most international student-friendly banks.

Will my university know I'm using StatusGuard?

▼

No. Your data is private and FERPA-protected. We never share information with your university unless you explicitly trigger the "SOS Mode" to alert your DSO about an urgent compliance issue.

Can I cancel anytime?

▼

Yes. No contracts, no cancellation fees. You can downgrade to the free tier or cancel completely from your account settings. Your data will be securely deleted within 30 days per GDPR requirements.
